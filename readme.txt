== Description ==

Spotlight is a sophisticated WordPress theme powered by FaustJS, styled with TailwindCSS, and supercharged with twin.macro. Designed for adaptability and elegance, Spotlight offers a seamless experience across various devices and browsers.

Whether you're a blogger, a business owner, or a creative professional, the Spotlight theme ensures a visually stunning presence online. Explore endless design possibilities and robust features with Spotlight, where innovation meets aesthetics.
